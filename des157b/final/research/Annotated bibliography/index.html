#2: "Unequal Childhoods: Annette Lareau" - Video by Annette Lareau (2016)

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6HN9ydNktAc

This is a short video by Lareau based on her research about the 12 kids she obsercer from different social class. Lareau she found out that all the parents had different ideas on how to raise a child. Then, Lareau goes into the differences that were noted between middle class and lower class children. It showed that middle class children were participating in organized activities while lower class children were able to be more creative but had more autonomy. However, middle class children had more valued skills which put them at an advantage because lower class children did not have this.

#3: "Social Class and the Hidden Curriculum of Work" - Article by Jean Anyon (1980)

https://www1.udel.edu/educ/whitson/897s05/files/hiddencurriculum.htm

The author Jean Anyon discusses examples of work tasks and interaction in five elementary schools in contrasting social class communities. The examples illustrate differences in classroom experience and curriculum knowledge among the schools.
