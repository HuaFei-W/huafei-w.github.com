Grays degree

I was inspired by the layout of this website. The site included a 3D gallery where students could display their work and also look at other students' work. Additionally, users can click the Courses menu and Category menu to access different disciplines they are interested in looking at. I think that is a good way to show the variety of the school's disciplines and also create a place for students to collaborate with other students. I plan to collect free resoureces from different areas in order to help students who have limited educational resoureces have the equal opportunities as other students.
